Our Story

Connecting people, moments, and ideas since 2010

Humble Beginnings

2010

Blames was founded in a college dorm room by two friends who wanted a better way to share photos and updates with their classmates. The first version let users post status updates and comment on friends' walls.

2 Founders
1K Early Users

Going Viral

2012

After launching a mobile app, Blames quickly spread across campuses nationwide. Viral features like photo tagging and group chats fueled rapid growth.

500K Users
10 Team Members

Series A & New Features

2014

Secured Series A funding and introduced the News Feed, Stories, and private messaging. Blames became a daily habit for millions.

5M Active Users
$8M Funding Raised

Global Expansion

2017

Launched in over 30 countries and added support for multiple languages. Blames became a platform for sharing moments worldwide.

50M Users
30+ Countries

Moments & Memories

2019

Introduced Moments, allowing users to share short videos and live streams. Blames became a hub for real-time experiences and creative expression.

120M Users
1B+ Moments Shared

Community & Safety

2021

Launched new privacy controls, content moderation tools, and community guidelines to foster a safe and positive environment for all users.

300M Users
99% Content Safety
